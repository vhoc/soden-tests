Project Management,
focused on sustainability

Staff at Soden Sustainability Consulting (SSC) works with a wide variety of stakeholders in the public, private, academic, and cultural institutional sectors. Our experience with green building is comprehensive, spanning all building types and aspects of the design and construction process. Our project management services help to customize the sustainability plan for your project based on your goals. Typically our clients are seeing some combination of the following:

  • LEED Services
  • Living Building Challenge Services
  • Green Guidelines and Standards
  • Implementation Strategies - Achieving High-Performance Buildings
  • Project based Education and Training

LEED Services

SSC has been designated by the Green Building Certification institute as a LEED Proven Provider. This a new designation that streamlines the LEED project review process for experienced organizations that demonstrate consistent excellence in delivering successful LEED projects. SSC has extensive experience with the LEED process including all rating systems (BD&C, CS, IDC, ND and O&M) and project types. We customize each project based on the needs of the team, to provide the necessary leadership, assistance and support to achieve LEED certification.

  • LEED Building Design & Construction

    • LEED BD+C: New Construction and Major Renovation.
    • LEED BD+C: Core and Shell Development

    • LEED BD+C: Retail

    • LEED BD+C: Warehouses and Distribution Centers
    • LEED BD+C: Hospitality

Sustainability Standards
for maximum efficiency

Staff at SSC have worked with many state and city agencies to help support their green building goals along with setting specific criteria for projects within those communities. SSC permitting services begin at project inception and follow through each step from early approvals, permitting and Certificate of Occupancy submissions. SSC also helps support projects that are not required to meet Green Criteria but want to set a sustainability plan that works for the team and project’s needs.  We meet you where you are and chart a course to where you want to be.
